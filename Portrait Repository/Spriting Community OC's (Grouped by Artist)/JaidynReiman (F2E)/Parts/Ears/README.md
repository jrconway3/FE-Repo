### Ear Variants (JaidynReiman)

These ears were designed by me, except for the short elven ears (they were AI generated and I traced/redrew them, then created the round ears based on these).

#### Elven Ears

There's various types of Elven Ears here. I think its nice to have a good variety. I may drop in additional angles later.

The hanging ears were originally just the Queiza ears, but I wanted something on them to stand out, so I made the Queiza variant.

The short elven ears resemble many variants of FE "dragon" type ears, or perhaps a "goblin" sort of ear.

#### Queiza Ears

"Queiza" is essentially a water elf-like race of mine. I don't expect anyone else to use these, but the option is available. The filled gap is intended to look like a webbing between the curves of the ear but I haven't exactly gotten down the design well enough yet.

#### Lykon Ears

Lykons are my wolf/canine style race. I don't do ears on top of the head, but instead they're a side ear. I will likely create several variants of this in the future, and will use these ears to create "generic" lykons from existing vanilla portraits.
